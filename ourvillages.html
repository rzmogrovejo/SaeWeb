Visit our villages

Socllabamba (Huancavelica)

Located in Huancavelica region, Socllabamba is a remote village in the province of Angaraes (13800 feet). With 20 households and around 120 people living there, the village main activity is the raising of Alpaca and sheep cattle. The community center phone is 05167 – 813507.

Latapuquio (Huancavelica)

Located in Huancavelica region, Latapuquio is a village in the province of Angaraes (12000 feet). With 70 families and over 300 people living in the village, the main productive activity of the villagers is the breeding of cows, sheep and other animals. The community center phone is 05167 - 813510.

Chepen (La Libertad)

Located in La Libertad region, Chepen is one of the most beautiful cities in the north of Peru. Known as the “Pearl of the North”. Chepen offers a range of tourism attractions that include: Colonial Temples, Ruins, Natural monuments, etc. The main economic activity is the rice, sugar cane and mango cultivation. For more details of the programs and sightseeing the municipality phone is 05144- 561202.

Chosica (Lima)

Located in Lima province, Chosica is a city located in the Rimac Valley. It offers a range of touristic activities, restaurants, trekking options, etc. For more information please visit: www.chosica.com , or contact the local municipality at: 0511 360-3075.

Patahuasi (Huancavelica)

Located at 25 Km from the district of Lircay in Huancavelica, Patahuasi is a village of 20 families in the remote area in the center of Peru. The main productive activity of the villagers is the breeding of cows and sheep and agriculture. For more information of the village and visits you can contact the local municipality of Lircay: 05167-458162.
