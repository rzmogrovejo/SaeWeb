Who we are

We are a social enterprise that promotes education and development in remote Andean communities through sustainable tourism. We develop electrification projects across remote areas using tourism programs as a way of financing the infrastructure and promoting those villages as tourism destinations.

Our Team

Our team includes different world-class professionals and experts in the creation of travelling experiences around the globe. In addition, our technical background allow us to develop and apply technology for the development of remote villages and rural communities in the Andean areas of Latin-American.

Vision

To be a global reference in the promotion and application of technology for rural development.

Mission

We generate experiences and projects in benefit of remote communities through innovative solutions that use renewable technology.
