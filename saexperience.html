The next expedition:

Cusco Program - SAE GLOBAL

Tour Highlights

Take an immersive journey through diverse regions of Peru, meeting locals and enjoying the natural beauty along the way, while supporting the electrification of remote villages.

Feel welcome throughout the journey in various homestays, where you will share meals and stories with local families.

Experience authentic Peruvian culture and tradition as you visit architectural sites, participate in religious ceremonies, and learn local handicrafts. Tour revenue will be invested in solar panel projects, which provide electricity to remote areas of Peru.

Tour Details

  • Duration: 10 days / 9 nights
  • Good For: Adults, Families, Youth, Students, Academics, Professionals, Couples, Young adults.
  • Languages: English, Spanish
  • Difficulty: Medium (2-5 miles/ 3-8 km walking distance, some physical effort per day)
  • Clothing & Equipment: Backpack, Camera, Comfortable shoes for walking, Hat, Headlamp/flashlight, Sunglasses, Sunscreen lotion
  • Tour Participants: 8 to 20

Tour Description

  • Day 1 - Cusco – Arrival to Cusco, Visit to the Youth Center and Bakery (local NGOs).
  • Day 2 - Cusco - Visit Ancient Archaeological Complexes.
  • Day 3 - Cusco – Travel to Urubamba Valley.
  • Day 4 - Cuncani - The Electrification Project. Experience the local way of living.
  • Day 5 - Cuncani - The Electrification Project. Solar panel installation.
  • Day 6 - Cuncani - The Electrification Project. Enjoying activities with the community.
  • Day 7 - Huayopata - Coffee Cultivation from Bean to Brew on route to Machu Picchu.
  • Day 8 - Aguas Calientes, Arrive to the small town of Aguas Calientes, next to Machu Picchu.
  • Day 9 - Machu Picchu & Cusco.
  • Day 10 - Cusco - Last day!
